Raging Tornado Damages 20 Houses in Tasikmalaya

  • 2 Min To Read
  • 16 Jan, 2024
    • City
    • Wind
    • Disaster

A tornado 'raged' in Tasikmalaya Regency. As many as 20 houses were damaged by the tornado. The raging tornado occurred in Bojongasih District and Rajapolah District, Tasikmalaya Regency on Tuesday (16/1/2024). Many houses were damaged in Toblongan Bojongasih Village and Tanjungpura Village, Rajapolah District. The roof and ceiling of the house were blown away by the tornado. The roofs of several houses also collapsed due to falling trees. It is known that apart from damaging houses, the tornado also uprooted a number of trees.

"The tornado hit at 15.30 WIB in the afternoon, suddenly the strong winds hit my house. It flew off and the roof collapsed. I saved myself," said one of the victims of the damaged house, Ilham. Tanjungpura Village Head of Community Welfare Andi said that at least 20 houses were damaged by the tornado. The 20 houses are in Tanjungpura Village, Rajapolah District. "There were 20 houses damaged, 17 houses were damaged while the rest were heavily damaged. There were no fatalities," said Andi. Meanwhile, two residents' houses in Toblongan Village, Bojongasih District, were damaged by falling trees after strong winds. Luckily, there were no casualties in this incident. "The reports that came to us were from two sub-districts, including Rajapolah and Bojong Asih. In Bojongasih there were two residents' houses that were damaged," said Jembar Adi Setia, Chair of FK Tagana, Tasikmalaya Regency.

The joint team is still making efforts to evacuate buildings and trees that have fallen on houses. "The team is still assessing damaged houses and assisting with evacuations. Thank God there have been no reports of casualties. Don't hope there aren't any," said Jembar. Meanwhile, the Tasikmalaya Regency BPBD Pusdalops is still assessing the amount of damage. "The report hasn't been submitted yet, officers are still in the field," said a Pusdalops BPBD Tasikmalaya officer.
