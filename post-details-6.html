Throughout 2023, there will be 4,940 natural disasters in Indonesia

  • 2 Min To Read
  • 12 Jan, 2024
    • Indonesia
    • Disaster

The National Disaster Management Agency delivered the 2023 Disaster Kaleidoscope and 2024 Disaster Outlook. BNPB recorded 4,940 natural disasters occurring throughout 2023.

Head of BNPB (National Disaster Management Agency) Lt. Gen. TNI Suharyanto delivered a press conference for the 2023 Disaster Kaleidoscope and 2024 Disaster Outlook at Graha BNPB, Jakarta, Friday (1/12/2024).
