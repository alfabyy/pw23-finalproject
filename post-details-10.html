Landslides in Limapuluh Cities, 1 Killed and West Sumatra-Riau Road Cut Off

  • 2 Min To Read
  • 26 Des, 2023
    • Riau
    • Disaster
    • Rain

Floods and landslides hit Limapuluh Kota Regency, West Sumatra (Sumbar). This natural disaster resulted in one person being killed and access to West Sumatra and Riau being cut off due to being covered by landslide debris. The Regent of Limapuluh Kota, Safaruddin, said there were 10 landslide locations. Currently the relevant parties are still cleaning up the material covering the road.

"Currently I am still at the scene of the incident, precisely in Lubuk Air-Koto Alam. There were 10 landslides due to this flood. One person was also reported to have died as a result of being carried away by the landslide. I still don't know his identity. However, for the West Sumatra-Riau route "It can't be passed at all until now," he said, Tuesday (26/12/2023). Apart from that, landslides also occurred in several sub-districts in Limapuluh Kota. Meanwhile, according to people heading to West Sumatra-Riau, there are still people trapped near the scene of the incident.

"There are a lot of people trapped as a result of this incident. However, one person was the victim," he said. The 10 landslide points, he said, experienced almost the same damage. "The damage in the 10 locations is almost the same. As a result, currently this causeway is still impassable," he explained. He advised people going to Riau to look for alternative routes. Because it is believed that cleaning the material will take a long time. "We continue to urge the public to be more careful. Because we predict that handling landslide material will take a very long time. If there is an urgent need to go to Riau, please look for an alternative route," he concluded.
